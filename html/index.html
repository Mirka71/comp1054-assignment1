According to Grunge, Blue Öyster Cult was initially formed under a different name in the 1960s by Albert Bouchard and Donald Roeser in New York. After years of adding members and renaming the band, they finally settled on Blue Öyster Cult in the 70s. furthermore, American Songwriter claimed that due to the culture around that time, they mainly focused on sci-fi themes and portrayed themselves as being esoteric and cult-like.
